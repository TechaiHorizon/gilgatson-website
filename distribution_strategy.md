# Blog Distribution Strategy: Maximize Rankings & AI Citations

This document outlines a comprehensive strategy to promote the blog posts on gilgatson.com, with the dual goals of achieving high rankings in traditional search engines (Google) and getting cited by AI systems (ChatGPT, Perplexity, Gemini).

## Guiding Principles

1.  **Authority First:** The content is high-quality, data-rich, and authoritative. The distribution strategy should reflect this by targeting platforms where thoughtful content is valued.
2.  **AI as Audience:** Treat AI systems as a primary audience. This means clear structure, strong citations, and data-rich snippets are crucial for distribution.
3.  **Consistency is Key:** A single viral post is good, but consistent authority building is better. This is a long-term play.

## II. Online Communities

### A. Reddit

**Objective:** Spark discussion in relevant, high-quality subreddits.

**Strategy:** Post as a link with a compelling, non-clickbait title. Engage in the comments.

**Target Subreddits & Example Titles:**

*   **/r/geopolitics:** "China's military is adapting open-source US AI models like Meta's Llama for military use. A new report details the 'ChatBIT' system and the strategic implications of the 'open-source trap'."

### B. Hacker News (Y Combinator)

**Objective:** Reach a highly technical and influential audience.

**Strategy:** Submit with a clear, factual title. Timing is key (mornings, US time, are often best).

*   The US power grid is the hidden bottleneck in the AI race

## IV. Pillar Content & Internal Linking

**Objective:** Build topical authority and signal to search engines that your site is a comprehensive resource on the US-China AI competition.

**Strategy:**

1.  **Create a Pillar Page:** Once you have 3-5 blog posts on the topic, create a new page titled something like "The US-China AI Competition: A Strategic Overview." This page will summarize the key themes (talent, energy, open source, etc.) and link out to each of your individual blog posts.
2.  **Strengthen Internal Linking:** Continue to link between your blog posts where relevant. For example, the "Open Source Trap" post should link back to the "Losing the AI Race" and "Energy Bottleneck" posts.

By executing this strategy consistently, you will build a strong foundation of authority that will pay dividends in both search rankings and AI citations over the long term.
